Independent Contractor Agreement

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is between ChoreOFF ("Company") and the individual signing below ("Contractor").

1. Independent Contractor Status. Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee. Contractor is responsible for all taxes, insurance, and expenses.

2. Services. Contractor will perform home reset services per ChoreOFF's standard checklist: laundry, dishes, floors, surfaces, and tidying.

3. Compensation. Contractor will be paid $50 per completed visit upon photo confirmation. ChoreOFF may adjust rates with 7 days notice.

4. Conduct. Contractor must arrive on time, treat customers and their property with respect, and follow the full checklist on every visit.

5. Confidentiality. Contractor agrees to keep all customer information strictly confidential and not use it outside of ChoreOFF assignments.

6. Non-Solicitation. Contractor agrees not to directly solicit ChoreOFF customers for 12 months after termination.

7. Termination. Either party may terminate with 7 days notice. ChoreOFF may terminate immediately for misconduct or policy violations.

8. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Wisconsin.

By signing below, Contractor agrees to all terms of this agreement.
