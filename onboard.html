Independent Contractor Agreement

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between ChoreOFF ("Company") and the individual signing below ("Contractor"). By signing, Contractor agrees to all terms stated herein.

1. Independent Contractor Status

Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of ChoreOFF. Contractor is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes on compensation received. ChoreOFF will not withhold income taxes, Social Security, or Medicare from payments. Contractor will receive a Form 1099-NEC for earnings of $600 or more in a calendar year.

2. Services

Contractor agrees to perform home reset services as assigned through the ChoreOFF platform, including laundry, dishes, floors and surfaces, and tidying. Contractor agrees to follow ChoreOFF's standard service checklist on every job. Services are limited to the defined scope — no deep cleaning, repairs, moving, or mounting.

3. Compensation

Contractor will be paid $50.00 USD per completed household visit, upon job completion and photo confirmation through the ChoreOFF worker portal. ChoreOFF reserves the right to adjust compensation rates with 7 days written notice.

4. Clothing & Property Damage

Contractor assumes responsibility for exercising reasonable care with customer property and clothing during laundry services. ChoreOFF is not liable for incidental clothing damage resulting from normal laundry operations. In cases of gross negligence, ChoreOFF reserves the right to deduct damages from Contractor compensation.

5. Discretionary Charges

For visits where the living quarters are found to be in an excessively unmaintained or overflowing state beyond normal scope, ChoreOFF reserves the right to assess a discretionary charge to the customer, which may be shared with the Contractor at ChoreOFF's discretion.

6. Conduct & Standards

Contractor must: arrive on time; treat customers and their property with respect; complete the full checklist on every visit; submit a completion photo; communicate promptly through the worker portal; not solicit customers directly outside of ChoreOFF.

7. Confidentiality

Contractor agrees to keep all customer information — including names, addresses, contact details, and home layouts — strictly confidential. This information may not be shared or used for any purpose outside of completing ChoreOFF assignments.

8. Non-Solicitation

During this agreement and for 12 months after termination, Contractor agrees not to directly solicit ChoreOFF customers for any competing home service.

9. Termination

Either party may terminate with 7 days written notice. ChoreOFF may terminate immediately for misconduct, policy violations, or repeated poor performance without notice or compensation.

10. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Wisconsin. Any disputes shall be resolved in Dane County, Wisconsin.
